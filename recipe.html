The Perfect PB&J

Description

A classic easy-yet-satisfying standby for when the munchies hit.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Directions

  1. Lay 2 slices of bread down on plate or flat surface.
  2. Using a butter/spreading knife, spread desired amount of peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  3. Repeat step 2 on the other slice of bread, but this time with jelly.
  4. Place on of the two halves on top of the other.
  5. (Optional) Cut in half, diagonally, or in quarters to eat in bite sizes or share with friends!
  6. Enjoy your sandwich!
